Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name and email address provided during registration.
  • Activity Data: Task completions, earnings, withdrawals, and other platform interactions.
  • Device Data: Basic browser and device information for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and maintain your account.
  • To process earnings and withdrawals.
  • To improve our platform and user experience.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not maintain external servers or databases. This means your data stays on your device and is not shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Your Choices

You can update your name at any time from your profile page. You can clear your data by clearing your browser's localStorage, but this will remove your account and earnings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out through the platform.
